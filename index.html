About Loopull & Me

Loopull is my personal crochet practice, focused on expressive plush characters and thoughtful, handcrafted details.

Meet Loopy, the mascot

Loopy represents the playful side of my work: cozy, circular, and always in motion.

How I started crocheting

I began crocheting as a way to make small gifts for friends. Over time it became a way to design characters with their own personality and story.

What I focus on

I specialize in amigurumi-style plushies, paying attention to shaping, proportion, and small details like accessories, facial expressions, and color combinations.

Skills I bring

Pattern interpretation and modification, custom character design, color matching, scaling plush sizes, and finishing work that feels polished and gift-ready.

My Crochet Process: From idea to plush

Every piece begins with a reference or idea. I decide on the character’s proportions, colors, and key details before I ever pick up the yarn.

1. Plan & Sketch

I sketch or gather visual references, then break the character into simple shapes (head, body, limbs, accessories) to plan how each part will be constructed.

2. Crochet & Shape

Using amigurumi techniques, I crochet each piece, adjusting stitch counts to shape features like snouts, ears, hats, or clothing so they match the design.

3. Assemble & Finish

After stuffing and assembling, I add details like embroidery, buttons, or color changes. The goal is to give each plush its own expression and personality.
